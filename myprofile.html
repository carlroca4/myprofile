About Me

I’m a 20-year-old student developer from Cuenca, Batangas, passionate about creating dynamic and responsive web applications. My journey in coding is driven by a desire to craft clean, efficient, and user-friendly solutions.

"Striving for excellence in every line of code"

My Background

I started my tech journey at 16 when I stumbled upon HTML and CSS while building a simple webpage for a school project. That sparked my love for coding, and I’ve since dived into languages like Python, JavaScript, and Java. Being an IT student has taught me the importance of problem-solving and creativity in development.

My Goals

As an aspiring web developer, my goal is to build innovative websites that not only look great but also provide seamless user experiences. I aim to master modern frameworks like React and explore backend development to become a full-stack developer in the next few years.
